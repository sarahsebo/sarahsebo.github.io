Prospective PhD Students

If you are a prospective graduate student, please apply to the UChicago CS PhD program. The application submission deadline for 2020 is December 15. Also, feel free to send me an email (sarahsebo@uchicago.edu) if you have any questions.

Current UChicago Sudents

If you are a current UChicago graduate or undergraduate student interested in working with me, please send me an email (sarahsebo@uchicago.edu) containing the following:

  • a few sentences on why you're interested in working with me
  • a copy of your academic transcript
  • your CV/resume
  • (if you have them) links to your online portfolio/website or repository profile (e.g., Github)
